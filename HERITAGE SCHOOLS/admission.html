Entry into Greensprings School is either by informal or more structured assessments, according to entry level.

Students seeking admission into Pre-School are assessed informally. More structured assessments are conducted for those pupils seeking entry into classes ranging from Year 1 to Year 10 in Mathematics and English. Students must fulfill the entry requirements for each class before they are offered admission. Admission to IB at Lekki campus is related to IGCSE performance in specific subjects.

Most successful applicants join at the start of the School year in September; occasionally students are admitted during the course of the school year based on availability.
