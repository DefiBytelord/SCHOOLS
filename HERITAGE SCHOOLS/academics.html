Academics

At Heritage Creativity Schools, learning is our primary focus, and we embrace a child-centered approach.

Our teaching and learning policy highlights the need for engaging, relevant, and challenging lessons to equip our students for life.

Quality teaching forms the foundation for personalized support, enabling each student to reach their full potential.

How we structure our learning:

CRECHE: 3 months and above

Primary School (5 to 10 years)

Our curriculum and assessment procedures take into account the British National Primary Framework. The Elementary School curriculum, in addition to the core subjects of English, mathematics and science, includes history, geography, information technology, music, art, physical education, French and Nigerian cultural studies (NCS). Our focus over the next academic year is to have standard assessment tests at Key stages 1, 2 and 3. Academic Structure

  • Study six subjects selected from six subject groups.
  • Complete an extended essay.
  • Engage in a Theory of Knowledge (TOK) course.
  • Participate in Creativity, Action, and Service (CAS).
  • Study three subjects at a higher level (240 teaching hours).
  • Study three subjects at a standard level (150 teaching hours).

Homework Policy

Homework is assigned regularly across all subjects, with increasing complexity as students progress.

To maintain high standards, we monitor the assignments closely.

Students are required to record their homework in personal planners, and we encourage parents to review them daily. Parental support is vitalâ€”by providing a quiet space, taking an interest in their child's work, and offering encouragement, they contribute to academic success.

Enrichment & Extra-Curricular Activities

Our enrichment and extracurricular programmes are diverse and immersive, fostering cross-disciplinary learning.

Students participate in an exciting range of sporting, creative, and adventure-based activities, including: The International Award for Young People Nigeria, Nottingham Football Tournament ,Kenyan Drama Road Show, Leadership Training Programme, Wizkid Science Trip (USA), Summer Olympic Trip (UK)
